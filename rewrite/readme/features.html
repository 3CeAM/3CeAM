Features

eAthena tries to keep up with kRO (Korean Ragnarok Online, an official server) in terms of features. Many of the features we have are re-coded versions of official onces. We also have many eAthena exclusive features, such as various NPC commands. We also have all of the RO server emu goodies, like open-source modification and custom sprites/items. We are currently on feature-lock, so we will focus mainly on stability for the time being, not new features.
  • PVP (Player Vs. Player)
  • Adoption System
  • Dynamic monster spawning system
  • GVG (Guild Vs. Guild)
  • qPets (Cute Pets)
  • Monster Skills
  • 2-2 Jobs (Alternate 2nd Jobs)
  • Super Novice (Alternate 1st Job)
  • WoE (War of Emperium)
  • Remote administration of accounts (ladmin)
  • Weddings
  • Pet equipped mobs
  • Management of day/night
  • Mob Disguises
  • Weather and other special effects
  • "Rebirth/Transcendent" Classes
  • Room for custom sprites/items
  • Open source, allowing for your own personal modifications
  • Two different storage systems, TXT and SQL
  • Stability
  • Many other various features
