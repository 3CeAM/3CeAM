NPC Features

One of the best aspects of eAthena is the NPC system. As a base, we have most of the official kRO NPC scripts, and if they are in iRO, we have their correct translations. You can put any of your custom NPCs on top of these, using our extremely easy-to-learn scripting language. We also have a wide variety of custom NPCs available in /npc/custom/ and for download in the forums to maximize your players' experiences.
